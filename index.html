AIM: To end up with just one marble.

You can remove a marble by jumping an adjacent marble over it and into a hole.
NO DIAGONAL MOVES!


STEPS TO PLAY:
[1] Click on any marble to select it.
[2] Click on a hole to move the marble. If possible, it will be done and you will score a 100 points for every successful move.
[3] Play ends when you are unable to move any marble. Press the "GAME OVER" button when this happens to stop the timer.
